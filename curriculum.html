Our Curriculum

Early Years

These classes are for the youngest children from 1.5 years to the age of three years. Nursery 1 provides a gentle and fun start allowing your child the opportunity to interact, gain independence and become accustomed to spending time away from parents and carers in a nurturing and caring environment in preparation for full time nursery.

Mornings are filled with plenty of singing, rhymes, role play and listening to stories to help children acquire language. Fun music lessons are provided, art and craft, painting and modelling bring out their creativity and help to improve their manipulative hand skills.

Primary School

We offer Primary level British curriculum education from Year 1-6. We are determined to effectively transition our children from the early years to KS1 (Year 1&2) as they progress to KS2 (Year 3-6) to mark the end of their primary level. We widely engage each student to realize their full potential in a child-centered approach and to grow holistically both academically and through extra-curricular and creative arts.

We aim to empower our kids to be able to fit anywhere in the world. Our able team focuses in making learning enjoyable for the students as they explore through the support subjects, which we treat equally as vital as the core subjects. We also strive at equipping our students with virtues like self-awareness, confidence and innovativeness as they transition from one stage to another.

PERSONAL,SOCIAL AND EMOTIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Children learn to be able to separate from their parent and enjoy exploring the fun environment of Arklight environment. The children learn to be part of a group and work cooperatively

PHYSICAL DEVELOPMENT

Our children learn to manage their own bodies through learning about healthy eating and using the toilet independently. Playing helps the children develop their gross motor skills.

COMMUNICATION AND LANGUAGE DEVELOPMENT

Our curriculum emphasizes hands-on activities and project-based learning, and drama is one of many ways to make learning fun and memorable for our children

EXPRESSIVE ART AND DESIGN DEVELOPMENT

Our children are encouraged to explore their creativity. Our children experiment with a range of imaginative resources and produce their own artistic creations, which we display with pride.

DEVOTION AND CIRCLE TIME ACTIVITIES

Kindergarten circle time is a time when the teachers and children gather together to start the day or work on things together. The children pray,memorize songs and poems, read stories and learn days of the week among other activities.
