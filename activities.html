SPORTS

Sports are an important part of life at Arklight, and our students have the opportunity to participate in a variety of sports that help them develop their mental, physical, and social well-being. We want to make sure that students enjoy physical activity and can compete in it safely Individual and team competitions are held. Our approach is based on the fact that students are educated on the value of sportsmanship

THE ARTS

Theatrical and musical productions are performed annually to ensure that students have an ongoing opportunity to practice their talents in theater, singing and dancing.The students are also to able to fully express themselves through fun activities such as painting

MUSIC

Here at Arklight International School we believe that music is a vital part of our lives. Arklight nurtures a culture of music appreciation and participation in our students from an early age and students are given the opportunity to acquire music skills throughout their time in the school
